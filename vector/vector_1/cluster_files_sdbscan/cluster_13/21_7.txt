Complete AMRAP with a partner in 20 minutes:
50 box jumps (20/24 in)
50 jumping pull-ups
50 KB swings (26/35 lb)
50 walking-lunge steps
50 knees-to-elbows
50 push presses (35/45 lb)
50 GHD hip extensions
50 wall-ball shots (14/20 lb) (9/10 ft)
50 burpees
50 double-unders
Notes:  Partner’s can share work as they see fit.
