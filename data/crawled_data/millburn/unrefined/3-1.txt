WOD: Saturday, March 1st, 2025 (Burpee Day 33)


Posted 
February 28, 2025
 by 
Diogo Dias
 
&
 filed under 
Workout of the Day
.


 






 




Warm-up: Group Dynamic




Plank – Push Up Tabata – 2 Minutes 


:20s Plank on hands – :10s of Push Ups 


Pigeon Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Pre-Game: 3 Rounds (10 Min Cap) 




1 Minute Row/Bike/Runner/Ski 


6 DB Hang Clean to Overheads (ascending weights) 


15 ft Bodyweight Walking Lunge 




*Focus on standards and movement quality


——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: CrossFit Open “25.1” 


Ladder x 15 Minutes 


3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, 21, 24, 27, 30, etc… 




Lateral Burpees Over DB 


DB Hang Clean to Overhead 


30 Ft Bodyweight Walking Lunge (15 ft out and back) 




*RX DB: (50/35) / Scaled (35/25)  


*Master 55+ (35/20) / Scaled M55+: (20/10) 


**STANDARDS**


Burpee
:




Must be done lateral to DB 


Chest and Thighs must be on the ground


Can step back into the burpee and up from the burpee


Must JUMP over DB – Stepping over not allowed 


Some part of your foot must pass over DB not around it




Hang Clean to Overhead: 




Can clean the first rep from the ground to start each set 


Every other rep must be from the hang (arm extended in hang) 


Can alternate arms whenever athlete wants 


DB must stop at the shoulder before being pressed overhead (no snatches) 


Knees, hips, elbow must be locked out at the top 


DB must be in line with the body 


Can use both hands to lower the DB but not to clean the DB 


Non working hand must NOT touch any part of the body 




Bodyweight Walking Lunge: 




Start each lunge by standing tall and behind the line 


Knee must make contact with the ground 


Alternating Legs is NOT necessary 


Knees and Hips MUST be extended at the top of each rep 


Both heels must cross the line for the lunge to count 


If you get a no rep during each 15 ft section you must repeat that section




——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 4 Rounds 




20 Weighted Reach the ceilings (25/15) 


20 Kick the ceilings




 


 


 






Today's WOD




WOD: Friday, May 16th, 2025 (Burpee Day 109)






Plank – Shoulder Tap Tabata – 2 Minutes 


:20s Plank on hands – :10s of Shoulder Taps 


Banded Shoulder Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Strength: Jerk (17 Minutes) 


4 Sets x 3 Reps 




Sets 1 and 2: 3 Reps each at 60% of 1RM 


Sets 3 and 4: 3 Reps each at 70% of 1RM 




*Make sure you stick with what you picked week 1 


*Focus on form and speed at these light weights 


*If you missed week 1 – test today 


——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Deadlifts (185/125) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




*CRX: 6 Wall Facing Handstand Push Ups (must start with a Wall walk)


*One score: total # of rounds + reps completed in 16 Minutes 


*Handstand Push up Scaling Options: 




Scale Reps OR Distance with Ab-Mats (NO KIPPING!) 


Plyo Box HSPU (6 Reps per round) 


Hand Release Push Ups (12 Reps per round) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Cardio Option: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Rage Balls (20/15) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 5 Rounds 




15 GHD Sit Ups 


15 GHD Back Extensions
