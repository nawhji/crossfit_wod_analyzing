WOD: Saturday, February 1st, 2025 (Burpee Day 5)


Posted 
January 31, 2025
 by 
Diogo Dias
 
&
 filed under 
Workout of the Day
.


 






 




Warm-up: Group Dynamic




Med-Ball Squat Tabata – 2 minutes


:20s bottom of squat hold – :10s of MB Squats  


Samson Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Pre-Game: 4 Rounds (12 Min Cap) 




Run to the end of the driveway 


3 Squat Cleans (ascending weights) 


3 Med-Ball Squats + 3 Wall Balls (at workout weight) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: For Time 




500 Meter Run




-into-


1 Round
 




12 Squat Cleans (115/85) 


24 Wall Balls (20/14) 




-Then-




500 Meter Run




-into-


2 Rounds 




6 Squat Cleans (135/95)


12 Wall Balls (20/14)




-Then-




500 Meter Run 




-into-


4 Rounds 




3 Squat Cleans (155/105) 


6 Wall Balls (20/14) 




*CRX: 12 @ (135/95), 6@ (155/105), 3@ (185/115) 


——————————————————————————————————–


Cardio Option: For Time 




500 Meter Run




-into-


1 Round
 




16 Atomic Sit Ups (25/15)  


24 Wall Balls (20/14) 




-Then-




500 Meter Run




-into-


2 Rounds 




8 Atomic Sit Ups (25/15) 


12 Wall Balls (20/14)




-Then-




500 Meter Run 




-into-


4 Rounds 




4 Atomic Sit Ups (25/15) 


6 Wall Balls (20/14) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Accessory Work: Bench Press




5 Sets x 3 Reps 




*Building across each set to a tough set of 3


*Aim to finish around 90% of 1RM 


——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 4 Rounds 




20 Weighted Reach the Ceilings (20/15) 


20 Kick the Ceilings 




 


 


 






Today's WOD




WOD: Friday, May 16th, 2025 (Burpee Day 109)






Plank – Shoulder Tap Tabata – 2 Minutes 


:20s Plank on hands – :10s of Shoulder Taps 


Banded Shoulder Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Strength: Jerk (17 Minutes) 


4 Sets x 3 Reps 




Sets 1 and 2: 3 Reps each at 60% of 1RM 


Sets 3 and 4: 3 Reps each at 70% of 1RM 




*Make sure you stick with what you picked week 1 


*Focus on form and speed at these light weights 


*If you missed week 1 – test today 


——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Deadlifts (185/125) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




*CRX: 6 Wall Facing Handstand Push Ups (must start with a Wall walk)


*One score: total # of rounds + reps completed in 16 Minutes 


*Handstand Push up Scaling Options: 




Scale Reps OR Distance with Ab-Mats (NO KIPPING!) 


Plyo Box HSPU (6 Reps per round) 


Hand Release Push Ups (12 Reps per round) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Cardio Option: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Rage Balls (20/15) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 5 Rounds 




15 GHD Sit Ups 


15 GHD Back Extensions
