WOD: Thursday, April 17th, 2025 (Burpee Day 80)


Posted 
April 16, 2025
 by 
Diogo Dias
 
&
 filed under 
Workout of the Day
.


 






 




Warm-up: Group Dynamic




Plate Squat Tabata – 2 Minutes 


:20s Bottom of squat hold – :10s of Plate Squats 


Couch Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Pre-Game: 4 Rounds 




1 Minute Row OR Bike 


3 Squat Cleans (ascending weights) 


:15s Hollow Hold 




——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: AMRAP Repeats 


AMRAP x 9 Minutes 




(16/12) Cal Row OR (12/8) Cal Bike 


16 Ab-Mat Sit Ups 


4 Squat Cleans (115/85) 




-Rest :90s- 


AMRAP x 7 Minutes 




(12/8) Cal Row OR (8/6) Cal Bike 


12 Ab-Mat Sit Ups 


3 Squat Cleans (135/95) 




-Rest :90s- 


AMRAP x 5 Minutes 




(8/6) Cal Row OR (6/4) Cal Bike 


8 Ab-Mat Sit Ups 


2 Squat Cleans (155/105) 




*CRX Weights: (135/95)(155/105)(185/125) 


*3 Total Scores:




Rounds + Reps for each AMRAP 




——————————————————————————————————–


Cardio Option: AMRAP Repeats 


AMRAP x 9 Minutes 




(16/12) Cal Row OR (12/8) Cal Bike 


16 Ab-Mat Sit Ups 


8 DB/KB Goblet Squats (50/35)(24K/16K)




-Rest :90s- 


AMRAP x 7 Minutes 




(12/8) Cal Row OR (8/6) Cal Bike 


12 Ab-Mat Sit Ups 


6 DB/KB Goblet Squats (50/35)(24K/16K)




-Rest :90s- 


AMRAP x 5 Minutes 




(8/6) Cal Row OR (6/4) Cal Bike 


8 Ab-Mat Sit Ups 


4 DB/KB Goblet Squats (50/35)(24K/16K)




——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 4 Rounds 




10 Banded Paloff Presses 


10 Banded Rotations




 


 


 






Today's WOD




WOD: Friday, May 16th, 2025 (Burpee Day 109)






Plank – Shoulder Tap Tabata – 2 Minutes 


:20s Plank on hands – :10s of Shoulder Taps 


Banded Shoulder Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Strength: Jerk (17 Minutes) 


4 Sets x 3 Reps 




Sets 1 and 2: 3 Reps each at 60% of 1RM 


Sets 3 and 4: 3 Reps each at 70% of 1RM 




*Make sure you stick with what you picked week 1 


*Focus on form and speed at these light weights 


*If you missed week 1 – test today 


——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Deadlifts (185/125) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




*CRX: 6 Wall Facing Handstand Push Ups (must start with a Wall walk)


*One score: total # of rounds + reps completed in 16 Minutes 


*Handstand Push up Scaling Options: 




Scale Reps OR Distance with Ab-Mats (NO KIPPING!) 


Plyo Box HSPU (6 Reps per round) 


Hand Release Push Ups (12 Reps per round) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Cardio Option: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Rage Balls (20/15) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 5 Rounds 




15 GHD Sit Ups 


15 GHD Back Extensions
