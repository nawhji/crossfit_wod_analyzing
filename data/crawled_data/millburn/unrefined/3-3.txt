WOD: Monday, March 3rd, 2025 (Burpee Day 35)


Posted 
March 2, 2025
 by 
Diogo Dias
 
&
 filed under 
Workout of the Day
.


 






 




Warm-up: Group Dynamic




Med-Ball Squat Tabata – 2 Minutes 


:20s Bottom of squat hold – :10s of MB Squats 


Couch Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Strength: Back Squat (17 Minutes) 




Sets 1 and 2: 5 Reps each at 75% of 1RM 


Sets 3 and 4: 3 Reps each at 80% of 1RM 




*Focus on bracing your core on every rep 


*Rest between each working set 


——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: For Time 


2 Rounds 




30 Wall Balls (20/14) 


15 Power Cleans (105/75) 




-Then-




1000 M Row / 800 M Run / 2400 M Bike 




-Then-


2 Rounds 




30 Box Jump Overs (24”/20”) 


15 Power Cleans (105/75) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Cardio Option: For Time 


2 Rounds 




30 Wall Balls (20/14) 


30 Rage Balls (20/15) 




-Then-




1000 M Row / 800 M Run / 2400 M Bike 




-Then-


2 Rounds 




30 Box Jump Overs (24”/20”) 


30 Rage Balls (20/15) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 4 Rounds 




15 Ab-Wheels 


30 Bicycle Crunches




 


 


 






Today's WOD




WOD: Friday, May 16th, 2025 (Burpee Day 109)






Plank – Shoulder Tap Tabata – 2 Minutes 


:20s Plank on hands – :10s of Shoulder Taps 


Banded Shoulder Stretch – 1 Minute per side 




——————————————————————————————————–


Strength: Jerk (17 Minutes) 


4 Sets x 3 Reps 




Sets 1 and 2: 3 Reps each at 60% of 1RM 


Sets 3 and 4: 3 Reps each at 70% of 1RM 




*Make sure you stick with what you picked week 1 


*Focus on form and speed at these light weights 


*If you missed week 1 – test today 


——————————————————————————————————–


Workout: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Deadlifts (185/125) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




*CRX: 6 Wall Facing Handstand Push Ups (must start with a Wall walk)


*One score: total # of rounds + reps completed in 16 Minutes 


*Handstand Push up Scaling Options: 




Scale Reps OR Distance with Ab-Mats (NO KIPPING!) 


Plyo Box HSPU (6 Reps per round) 


Hand Release Push Ups (12 Reps per round) 




——————————————————————————————————–


Cardio Option: For Time 




Buy in: 800 Meter Run




-then complete 5 Rounds of:




6 Strict Handstand Push Ups 


12 Rage Balls (20/15) 


(12/8) Cal Row/Ski OR (8/6) Cal Bike 




——————————————————————————————————–


Core: 5 Rounds 




15 GHD Sit Ups 


15 GHD Back Extensions
