Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 8 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 40 Wall Balls 14/20

30 Pull Ups

30 Wall Balls

20 Chest to Bar Pull Ups

20 Wall Balls

10 Bar Muscle Ups


KODIAK
– 
30 Wall Balls 14/20
25 Pull Ups
20 Wall Balls 
15 Chest to bar Pull Ups
15 Wall Balls 
5 Bar Muscle Ups


POLAR
–
30 Wall Ball  (light)
20 Ring Rows
20 Wall Ball (light)
15 Bent Over Rows
15 Wall Ball (light)
10 Plank Pull Through (each side) 


Leg Day- 3 Rounds

10 Alternating Double DB Step Back Lunges (Front Rack) (each side)

10 Band Pull Through

15 Standing Barbell (Or Dumbbell) Calf Raise


-Rest 2 min between rounds-
