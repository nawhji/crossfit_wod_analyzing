Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 4 2024
 


GRIZZLY
– 14:00 AMRAP

14/11 Calorie Row

14 Deadlifts 105/155 M80/115

28 Crossovers


KODIAK
– 14:00 AMRAP

10/12 Calorie Row

12 Deadlifts 95/135 M75/105

18 Crossovers  OR 36 Double Unders


POLAR
– 14:00 AMRAP

8/10 Calorie Row

10 Dumbbell Deadlifts (light)

28 Single Unders


GYMNASTICS
: Core (Day 1) Every 1:00 (8:00)

Odd minute: 30 seconds of Hollow Rocks (OR Hollow Hold)

Even minute: 30 seconds of Strict Hanging Knee Raises
