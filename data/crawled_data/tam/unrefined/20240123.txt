Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 23 2024
 


5 Deadlifts x 5 sets @70% of Heavy Single (Week 1)

* Complete a set on the 2 minutes *


GRIZZLY
– 4 Sets- 2:00 AMRAP

175/200m Row

12 Box Jump Overs (20)

Max Thrusters 65/95 M55/75

-rest 2:00 between sets-


KODIAK
-4 Sets- 2:00 AMRAP

175/200m Row

10 Box Jump Overs (20)

Max Thrusters 55/75 M45/65

-rest 2:00 between sets-


POLAR
– 4 Sets- 2:00 AMRAP

150/175m Row

10 Box Step Ups (20)

Max Dumbbell Thrusters (light)

-rest 2:00 between sets
