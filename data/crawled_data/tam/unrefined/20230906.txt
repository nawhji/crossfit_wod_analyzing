Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 6 2023
 


5 sets: 1 High Hang Clean + 1 Hang Clean + 1 Clean (@75% of 1RM Clean & Jerk)

* Complete a set every 1:30 *

-into-

3 sets: 3 Push Jerks (@80% of 1RM Clean and Jerk)

* Rest as needed between sets *


GRIZZLY
– 126ft Front Rack Double Dumbbell Walking Lunge 35/50 M25/40

-into-

3 Rounds

2 Rope Climbs or 8 Strict Pull-ups

12 DB Clean and Jerks 35/50 M25/40

42ft Front Rack Double Dumbbell Walking Lunge 35/50 M25/40


KODIAK
–
126ft Front Rack Dumbbell Walking Lunge 
25/35 M20/30
-into-
3 Rounds
1 Rope Climb or 4 strict pull-ups
12 Dumbbell Clean and Jerks 25/35 M20/30
42ft Front Rack Dumbbell Walking Lunge 
25/35 M20/30


POLAR
– 
126ft Walking Lunge
-into-
3 Rounds
3 Zombie Rope Climbs (or 8 ring rows)
12 Alternating Dumbbell Clean and Jerks (light)
42ft Walking Lunge
