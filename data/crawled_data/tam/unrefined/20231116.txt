Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 16 2023
 


5 Deadlifts Every 2:00 x 5 sets (@70% of 1RM)


GRIZZLY
– 4 sets, with a new set starting every 4 minutes.

At 0:00 & 8:00- 3:00 Amrap

24/30 Calorie Row

Max Bench Press 80/115

-rest 1:00-

At 4:00 & 12:00- 3:00 Amrap

24/30 Calorie Row

Max Push Ups

-Rest 1:00-

*Score all bench reps as score 1 and all push up reps as score 2

-Rest 1:00-
