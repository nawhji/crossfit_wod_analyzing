Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 28 2023
 


5 sets x 3 Deadlifts @80% of 1RM

* Complete a set on the 2 minutes *


 GRIZZLY- 
5 Sets (1 set every 2:30)

8x50ft Shuttle Runs

12 Front Squats 95/135 M75/105


KODIAK
–
5 Sets (1 set every 2:30)
8x50ft Shuttle Runs
12 Front Squats 80/115 M65/95


POLAR
– 
5 Sets (1 set every 2:30)
6x50ft Shuttle Runs
12 Dumbbell Front Squats (light)
