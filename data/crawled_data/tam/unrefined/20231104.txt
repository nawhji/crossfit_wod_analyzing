Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 4 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– AMRAP- 20 Minutes

150’ Dumbbell Farmer Carry 50/70

20/25 Calorie Row or 200 M Run

150’ Lunge Walk


KODIAK
– AMRAP- 20 Minutes

150’ Dumbbell Farmer Carry 35/50

20/25 Calorie Row or 200 M Run

150’ Lunge Walk


POLAR
– AMRAP- 20 Minutes

150’ Dumbbell Farmer Carry light

20/25 Calorie Row or 200 M Run

50 Box Step Ups


DBL UNDER FUN
– For time, 15 minute cap:

GRIZZLY option:

5-10-15-20-25-30-35-40-45-50-45-40-35-30-25-20-15-10-5:

Unbroken Double Unders

KODIAK option:

10-20-30-40-50-40-30-20-10

Unbroken Double-Unders

POLAR option:

10-20-30-40-50-40-30-20-10

Unbroken Single-Unders


ACCESSORY
– 4 sets:

10 Single Arm KB Situps Left

10 Single Arm KB Situps R

10 Barbell Roll-Outs

30-60 second Plank Hold (elbows)

30yd Kettlebell Waiter Carry

*Rest 2:00 b/t sets
