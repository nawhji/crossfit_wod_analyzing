Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 14 2023
 


Please jump high with tired legs :)


GRIZZLY
– 3 rounds

10 Back Squats 135/185 M95/140

100ft Walking Lunge

10 Box Jumps 24/30


KODIAK
– 3 rounds

10 Back Squats 115/165

100ft Walking Lunge

10 Box Jumps 20/24


POLAR
– 3 rounds

10 Back Squats (light)

50ft Walking Lunge

10 Box Step Ups


AFTER PARTY
– 4 Rounds

10 Bent Over Barbell Row –Underhand Grip

12 Barbell Skull Crushers
