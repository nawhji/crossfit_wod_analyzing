Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 20 2024
 


GRIZZLY
– 3 Sets- 15-10-5

Handstand Push Ups

25ft Dumbbell Front Rack Walking Lunge 35/50 M25/40 after each set of Handstand Push-ups*

Rest 1:1


KODIAK
– 3 sets  12-8-4

Handstand Push Ups

25ft Dumbbell Front Rack Walking Lunge 35/50 M25/40 after each set of Handstand Push-ups*


POLAR
– 3 sets

15-10-5

Dumbbell Push Press (light)

25ft Lunge after each set *


GYMNASTICS:
 Core Day 2:

Advanced: EMOM for 6 minutes-

14 Alternating V Ups

Intermediate: EMOM for 6 minutes

12 Alternating V Ups

Novice: EMOM for 6 minutes

8-10 Alternating V Ups
