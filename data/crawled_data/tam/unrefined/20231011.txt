Tamalpais CrossFit WOD October 11 2023
 


Back Squat: – Establish a 10 RM for the day. Then, perform a drop set of 8-10 reps at 90% and 8-10 reps 80% of the original 10RM.


GRIZZLY
– AMRAP 15 Minutes

20 Deadlifts 80/115 M65/95

50 Double Unders

100ft Walking Lunge

50 Double Unders

10 Hang Power Cleans 80/115 M65/95


KODIAK
– 
AMRAP 15 Minutes
20 Deadlifts 65/95 M55/75
35 Double Unders
100ft Walking Lunge
35 Double Unders
10 Hang Power Cleans 
65/95 M55/75


POLAR
– 
AMRAP 15 Minutes
10 Dumbbell Deadlifts (light)
30 Single Unders
50ft Walking Lunge
35 Single Unders
10 Dumbbell Hang Power Cleans (light)
