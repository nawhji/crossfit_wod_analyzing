Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 25 2024
 


5 Bench Press x 5 sets @70% of Heavy Single (Week 1)

* Complete a set on the 2 minutes *


GRIZZLY
– 3 Rounds

30 Kettlebell Swings 35/53

30 Push Ups


KODIAK
-3 rounds

30 Kettlebell Swings 26/35

30 Push Ups


POLAR
– 3 rounds

20 Russian Kettlebell Swings (light)

20 Bar Push Ups


GYMNASTICS:
 Bar Muscle Up Prep (Day 1)


4 Positions of the Bar Muscle Up:

Position 1: Jump into Pike

Position 2: Arch

Position 3: Hollow Rock Around

Position 4: Support


Warm Up:

Advanced: 5 Reps of Position 1, 5 Reps of Position 1 & 2, 5 Reps of Positions 1,2 &3, 5 Reps of Bar Muscle Ups

Intermediate: 5 Reps of Position 1- 5 Reps of Position 1 & 2- 5 Reps of Positions 1,2,&3

Novice: 10 Kip Swings 5 Strict Pull Ups OR 5 Ring Rows


WORKOUT:

Advanced: Every 1:00 (6:00) 3 Bar Muscle Ups


Intermediate: Every 1:00 (6:00) 1 Bar Muscle Up + 2 Strict Chest To Bar Strict


Novice: Every 1:00 (6:00) 1-3 Box Bar Muscle Ups OR 3-5 Kipping Chest to Bar Pull Ups OR 3 Pull-up Negatives
