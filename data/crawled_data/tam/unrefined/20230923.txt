Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 23 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 2:00 Amrap

2 Rope Climbs Or 8 Strict pull Ups

100 M Shuttle sprint

Max Rep Back Squats 95/135 M75/105

-rest 1:00 between sets-

*4th round and on extend to 3 minutes.

Go until 75 reps of Back Squats are complete.


KODIAK
– 
2:00 Amrap
1 Rope Climb or 4 strict pull ups
110 M Shuttle Sprint
Max Rep Back Squats 65/95 M55/75
-rest 1:00 between sets-
4th round and on extend to 3 minutes
Go until 75 reps of Back Squats are complete


POLAR
– 
2:00 Amrap
10 Jumping Pull Ups
8 Calorie Row
Max Rep Air Squats
-rest 1:00 between sets-
4th round and on extend to 3 minutes
Go until 75 reps of Air Squats are complete


Arms and Shoulders- 4 Rounds

10 Barbell Strict Press

12 Plate Front Raise

10 DB Curls

10 Standing Tricep DB French Press

-Rest 3 min b/t rounds-
