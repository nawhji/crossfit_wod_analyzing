Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 1 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– AMRAP 20 Minutes

400ft Shuttle Run (16x25ft)

100ft Walking Lunge

25ft Handstand Walk


KODIAK
– 
AMRAP 20 Minutes
400ft Shuttle Run (16x25ft)
100ft Walking Lunge
3 Wall Walks


POLAR
– 
AMRAP 20 Minutes
300ft Shuttle Run (12x25ft)
50ft Walking Lunge
25ft Bear Crawl


ACCESSORY
 –

Barbell Curl 21’s

3 sets: 21 reps – RPE 8

*Rest 1:00-1:30 b/t sets
