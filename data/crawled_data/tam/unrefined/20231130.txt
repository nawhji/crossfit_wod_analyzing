Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 30 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 3 Rounds

24/30 Calorie Row

30 Kettlebell Swings 35/53


KODIAK
– 
3 Rounds
20/25 Calorie Row
30 Kettlebell Swings 26/35


POLAR
– 
3 Rounds
16/20 Calorie Row
20 Russian Kettlebell Swings (light)


CORE WORK
– 4 sets:

30 Heel Taps (each side)

30 yd Iso DB Farmers Carry (left)

30 yd Iso DB Farmers Carry (right)
