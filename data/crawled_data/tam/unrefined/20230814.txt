Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 14 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 21-15-9

Thrusters 65/95 M55/85

Burpee Over Bar

Chest to Bar


** Repeat from 7/25/22 **


KODIAK
–
21-15-9
Thrusters 55/75 M45/65
Burpee over Bar
Pull-ups


POLAR
–
21-15-9
Dumbbell Thrusters (light)
Sprawls
Ring Rows or Jumping Pull-ups


SKILLS AND DRILLS
 -Toes to Bar: Week 2, Day 1

Advance: 5 sets (10:00)

8-10 Kip Swings

5-8 Strict Toes to Bar

Intermediate: 5 sets (10:00)

6-8 Kip Swings

5-8 Strict Knee Raise +Extension

Beginner:

5 sets (10:00)

5 Kip Swings (Stand on tiptoes if needed)

5-8 Strict Hanging Knee Raise

Alternative Option:

5 sets (10:00)

10 Weighted Sit Ups

30-second Plank Hold

(elbows)
