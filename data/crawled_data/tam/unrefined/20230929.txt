Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 29 2023
 


Heavy 10-rep Back Squat


GRIZZLY
– Teams of 2

300 Box Step-ups (20” all athletes)

* Every 2:00 (including 0:00) perform 4 Power Cleans 135/185 M95/140

* Share the 300 box step ups between partners. Each athlete does 2 cleans each time.


KODIAK
-Teams of 2

300 Box Step-ups (20” all athletes)

* Every 2:00 (including 0:00) perform 4 Power Cleans 95/135 M75/115

* Share the 300 box step ups between partners. Each athlete does 2 cleans each time.


POLAR
– 
Teams of 2
200 Box Step-ups
*Every 2:00 (including 0:00) perform 4 Power Cleans- DB or BB
