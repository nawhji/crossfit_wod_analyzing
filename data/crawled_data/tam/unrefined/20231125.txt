Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 25 2023
 


GRIZZLY-
4 sets:

30 Dumbbell Box Step Ups 35/50 M25/40 – 20/24”

20 Hang Alternating Dumbbell Snatch 35/50 M25/40

-rest 1:1 between sets-


KODIAK
– 4 sets:

30 Dumbbell Box Step Ups 25/35 M20/30 – 20/24”

20 Hang Alternating Dumbbell Snatch 25/35 M20/30

-rest 1:1 between sets-


POLAR
– 
4 sets:
30 Single Dumbbell Box Step Ups (light)
30 Hang Alternating Dumbbell Snatch (light)


OPTION 1: GYMNASTICS – 
Strict Pull-up: Week 2 Day 2


Advanced: 3 rounds of:

20 Scap Pulls [Hanging Shrugs] into 20 second Dead Hang Followed by 5 Strict or Box Assist Pull Ups

–rest 90 seconds between sets–

Intermediate: 3 rounds of:

10 Scap Pulls [Hanging Shrugs] into 10 second Dead Hang Followed by 3-5 Strict or Box Assist Pull Ups

–rest 90 seconds between sets–

Beginner: 3 rounds of:

10 Scap Pulls [Hanging Shrugs] into 5 second Dead Hang Followed by 2 Box Assist Pull Ups

–rest 90 seconds between sets–


Or Fun Times 4 sets:

10 Strict TTB

10 KB Side Bend (each side)

10 GHD Russian Twists

1 min Plank

30yd Single DB Overhead Carry Left– heavy as possible

30yd Single DB Overhead Carry Right-heavy as possible

*Rest 2:00 b/t sets
