Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 29 2024
 


5 sets of 5 Deadlifts @75% of Heavy Single (Week 1)

* Complete on the 2 min x 10 minutes.


GRIZZLY
– 2 RNDS – On A 2 min clock

12/15 Cal Row

15 Box Jump Overs 20/24

2 min Rest

2 RNDS – On A 2 min clock

12/15 Cal Row

45 Crossovers

2 Min Rest


KODIAK
– 2 RNDS – On A 2 min clock

10/12 Cal Row

12 Box Jump Overs 20/24

2 min Rest

2 RNDS – On A 2 min clock

10/12 Cal Row

45 Double Unders

2 Min Rest


POLAR
– 2 RNDS – On A 2 min clock

8/10 Cal Row

10 Step Ups

2 min Rest

2 RNDS – On A 2 min clock

8/10 Cal Row

45 Single Unders

2 Min Rest
