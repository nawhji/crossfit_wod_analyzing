Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 4 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– For Time

50 Power Snatch 55/75 M45/65

50 Alt DB P Snatch

*Every minute, including 0:00, complete 15 Air Squats

– Complete the barbell snatch first, then move on to the DB


KODIAK
– For Time

50 Power Snatch 45/65 M35/55

50 Alt DB P Snatch

*Every minute, including 0:00, complete 12 Air Squats

Complete the barbell snatch first, then move on to the DB


POLAR
– For Time

100 Dumbbell Snatch (light)

*Every minute, including 0:00, complete 10 Air Squats


ACCESSORY
 – RPE 8- 4 sets: 10 reps (each side)

Bottom-Up Single Arm Standing KB Press

Leaning Lateral Raise

*Rest 1:00-1:30 b/t sets
