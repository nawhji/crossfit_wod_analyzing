Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 26 2023
 


7am, 8am & 9:30 am classes today!


GRIZZLY
– 4 sets

18 Pull Ups

50ft Single Dumbbell

Overhead Walking Lunge 35/50 M25/40

12 Single Arm Alternating Devil’s Press 35/50 M25/40

50ft Single DumbbellOH Walking Lunge 35/50 M25/40

-rest 1:1 between sets-


** Repeat from Tues Nov 23, 2021


KODIAK
– 4 sets

14 Pull Ups

50ft Single Dumbbell Walking Lunge 25/35 M20/30

12 Single Arm Alternating Devil’s Press 25/35 M20/30

50ft Single Dumbbell Walking Lunge 25/35 M20/30

-rest 1:1 between sets-


POLAR
– 4 sets

16 Jumping Pull Ups

50ft Walking Lunge

12 Single Arm Alternating Clean and Jerks (light)

50ft Walking Lunge

-rest 1:1 between sets-
