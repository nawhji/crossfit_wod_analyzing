Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 2 2023
 


Back Squat: – Establish a 4 RM for the day.

Then, perform a drop set of 2-4 reps at 85-90% and 2-4 reps 80-85% of the original 4RM.


GRIZZLY
– 3-person team- 16:00 AMRAP

Partner 1: 10 Deadlifts 125/185 M90/130

Partner 2: 10 Pull-ups

Partner 3: 10-12-14-16-18-20…Calorie Bike

(female calories: 7-9-11-13-15-17…)


KODIAK
–
3-person team- 
16:00
Partner 1: 10 Deadlifts 105/155 M80/115
Partner 2: 8 Pull-ups
Partner 3: 8-10-12-14-16-18… Calorie Bike
(female calories: 5-7-9-11-13-15…)


POLAR
– 
3-person team- 
16:00
Partner 1: 10 Dumbbell Deadlifts (light)
Partner 2: 10 Ring Rows
Partner 3: 8 Calorie Bike
