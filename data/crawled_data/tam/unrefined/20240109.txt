Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 9 2024
 


Every 1:00 for 10:00- 5 Clean and Jerks (touch and go)

*Use a light and comfortable weight- Rest until minute 12:00 (after strength)

From minute 12-22: Every minute on the minute for 10


GRIZZLY
– 175/200m Row


KODIAK
– 150/175m Row


POLAR
– 125/150m Row


GYMNASTICS: Core

Advanced:

Accumulate 80 V Ups Every time you break, perform 5 Burpees

Intermediate:

Accumulate 50 V Ups Every time you break, perform 5 Burpees

Novice:

Accumulate 50 Abmat Sit ups Every time you break, perform 3 Burpees
