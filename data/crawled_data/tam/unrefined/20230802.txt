Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 2 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– Every 3:00 (8 sets)

10 Burpee Over Bar

4 Squat Cleans 125/185 M90/130

10 Box Jump Overs 20/24


KODIAK
– 
Every 3:00 (8 sets)
8 Burpee Over Bar
4 Squat Cleans 105/155
 M80/115
10 Box Jump Over 16/20


POLAR
– 
Every 3:00 (8 sets)
10 Up Downs
8 Dumbbell Squat Cleans (light)
10 Box Step Ups




ACCESSORY
 -4 sets: 10 reps – RPE 8

Lying DB Hamstring Curl

Bent Over Barbell Row –Underhand Grip

*Rest 1:00-1:30 b/t sets
