Tamalpais CrossFit WOD October 13 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 2:00 Amrap (6 sets)

15/20 Calorie Air Bike

Max Distance Farmers Carry 50/70 M40/60

-Rest 1:00 Between sets-


KODIAK
–
2:00 Amrap (6 sets)
15/12 Calorie Air Bike
Max Distance Farmers Carry 35/50 M25/40
-Rest 1:00 between sets –


POLAR
– 
2:00 Amrap (6 sets)
1:00 Air Bike
Max Distance Farmers Carry 
-Rest 1:00 between sets –


SKILLS AND DRILLS
 
Double-Unders
: Week 3 Day 1

One Double-Under Followed by Single-Unders: Now we’re going to work on doing that first double-under and then keeping the rope spinning for some additional single-unders.

Goal Workout:

10 rounds:

3 single-unders

1 double-under

Once you can successfully complete this workout unbroken (with no misses), you’re ready for Phase 4.

Advanced Athlete Option:

If you already have mastered double-unders, instead, use this time to work on Crossovers.
