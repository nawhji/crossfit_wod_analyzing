Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 17 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– Open 17.2 -12:00 AMRAP

2 Rounds of:

50ft Dumbbell Walking Lunges 35/50 M25/40

16 Toe-to-Bars

8 Dumbbell Power Cleans

Then, 2 Rounds of:

50ft DB Walking Lunges

16 Bar Muscle-Ups

8 DB Power Cleans


KODIAK
– 
12:00 AMRAP
2 Rounds of:
50ft Dumbbell Walking Lunges 25/
35 M20/30
16 Toe-to-Bars
8 Dumbbell Power Cleans 
Then, 2 Rounds of:
50ft Dumbbell Walking Lunges 
10 Bar Muscle-Ups
8 Dumbbell Power Cleans 


POLAR
– 
12:00 AMRAP
2 Rounds of:
50ft Walking Lunges
16 Hang Knee Raises
8 Dumbbell Power Cleans (light)
Then, 2 Rounds of:
50ft Walking Lunges
16 Jumping Pull Ups
8 Dumbbell Power Cleans (light)
