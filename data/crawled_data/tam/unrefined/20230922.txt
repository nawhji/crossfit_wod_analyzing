Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 22 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 5 sets – rest 1:1

5 Burpee Deadlifts 35/50 M25/40

12/15 Calorie Assault Bike

5 Burpee Deadlifts


KODIAK
– 5 sets – rest 1:1

5 Burpee Deadlifts 25/40 M20/30

7/9 Calorie Assault Bike

5 Burpee Deadlifts


POLAR
– 
10:00 Amrap
5 Sprawls
10 Dumbbell Deadlifts (light)
8/10 Calorie Assault Bike


Fun times
– Chest and Triceps 4 Rounds

12 Chest Fly

10 Barbell Skull Crushers
