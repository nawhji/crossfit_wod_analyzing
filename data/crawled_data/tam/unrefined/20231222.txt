Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 22 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 12:00 AMRAP

10 Dumbbell Bench or Floor Press 35/50 M25/40

10 Dumbbell Deadlifts 35/50 M25/40


KODIAK
– 12:00 AMRAP

10 Dumbbell Bench or Floor Press 25/35 M20/30

10 Dumbbell Deadlifts 25/35 M20/30


POLAR
– 12:00 AMRAP

10 Dumbbell Bench Press (light)

10 Dumbbell Deadlifts (light)


Core Work:
 3 sets:

30 Heel Taps – AKA- Penguin (each side)

30 GHD Russian Twist (Or Regular Russian Twist)

30 yd Isolateral DB Farmers Carry (left)

30 yd Isolateral DB Farmers Carry (right)

*Rest 2:00 b/t sets
