Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 15 2023
 


Heavy 2-rep Deadlift

* Rest a little longer than normal between sets when you reach heavier weights *


GRIZZLY
– 6:00 Amrap

10 Push Ups

1 Rope Climb Or 4 Strict Pull-Ups

-2:00 Rest-

6:00 AMRAP

10 Dumbbell Bench Press 35/50 M25/40

5 Strict Pull Ups


KODIAK
–
6:00 Amrap
8 Push Ups
1 Rope Climb Or 4 Strict Pull Ups
-2:00 Rest-
6:00 AMRAP
10 Dumbbell Bench Press 25/35 M20/30
5 Strict Pull Ups


POLAR
– 
6:00 Amrap
10 Bar Push Ups
4 Jumping Pull Ups
-2:00 Rest-
6:00 AMRAP
10 Dumbbell Bench Press (light)
5 Ring Rows
