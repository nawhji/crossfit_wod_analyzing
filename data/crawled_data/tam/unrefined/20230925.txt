Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 25 2023
 


Heavy Single Snatch- spend some quality time building to a quality heavy snatch.


GRIZZLY
– 1750/2000m Row or 1600 M Run

Every 2:00 (including 0:00) perform 12/15 Push Ups


KODIA
K-
1500/1750m Row
Every 2:00 (including 0:00) perform 10/12 Push Ups


POLAR
– 
1200/1000m Row
