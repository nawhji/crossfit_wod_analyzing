Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 29 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– For Time:

150 Kettlebell Swings 35/53

*Every time you stop, break, put the kettlebell down, perform 10 Burpee


*This is a repeat from January 15th, 2023


KODIAK
– For Time:

150 Kettlebell Swings (35/25)

Every time you stop, break, put the kettlebell down, perform 8 Burpee


POLAR
– For Time:

100 Russian Kettlebell Swings (light)

Every time you stop, break, put the kettlebell down, perform 6 Sprawls


AFTER PARTY
– 4 Rounds

10 Weighted Hip Thrust

10 GHD Hip Extension (Or 15 Superman)

10 Plate Front Raise
