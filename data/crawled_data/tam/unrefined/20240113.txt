Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 13 2024
 


GRIZZLY
– Teams of 2

60 Bench Press 85/135 M65/105 (split as needed)

800m Run (together)

40 Bench Press 95/155 M75/110 (split as needed)

800m Run (together)

20 Bench Press 105/185 M80/135 (split as needed)

800m Run (together)

* Substitute 75/100 Calorie Air Bike (shared) in place of each run, if needed.


KODIAK
– Teams of 2

60 Bench Press 75/115 M60/95

500m Run (together)

40 Bench Press 85/135 M65/105

500m Run (together)

20 Bench Press 95/155 M75/110

500m Run (together)


POLAR
– Teams of 2

40 Dumbbell Bench Press (light)

300m Run (together)

30 Dumbbell Bench Press (light)

300m Run (together)

20 Dumbbell Bench Press (light)

300m Run (together)


GYMNASTICS
: Core

Advanced: Accumulate 40 Strict Toes To Bar Every time you break, 5 Sprawls

Intermediate: Accumulate 25 Strict Toes To Bar Every time you break, 5 Sprawls

Novice: Accumulate 25 Tuck Ups Every time you break, 3 Sprawls
