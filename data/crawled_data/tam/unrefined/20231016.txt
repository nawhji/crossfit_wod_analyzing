Tamalpais CrossFit WOD October 16 2023
 


Hang Squat Clean: 3RM

– Clean Grip Deadlift: 3×3 @ 90% of 1RM Clean


GRIZZLY
– 2 Sets:

3 Rounds

12 Thrusters 45/65 M35/55

12 GHD Sit Ups Or V-Ups

24 Crossover Single Unders

Or 48 Double Unders

-rest 3:00 between sets-


KODIAK
–
2 Sets:
3 Rounds
12 Thrusters 35/55
12 GHD Sit Ups + 6in riser
36 Double Unders
-rest 3:00 between sets-


POLAR
– 
2 Sets:
3 Rounds
12 Dumbbell Thrusters (light)
12 Sit Ups
24 Single Unders
-rest 3:00 between sets-
