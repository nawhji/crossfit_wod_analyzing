Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 18 2023
 


5 sets:- 3 Position Clean (high hang/hang/floor) @60- 70% of 1RM Clean & Jerk,

complete a set every 1:30

3 sets: 1 Push Jerk: @80-90% of 1RM Clean and Jerk


GRIZZLY
– 12-9-6

Hang Squat Clean 105/155 M80/115

Burpee Chest to Bar


KODIAK
–
12-9-6
Hang Squat Clean 95/135 M75/115
Burpee Pull Up


POLAR
– 
12-9-6
Hang Dumbbell Squat Clean (Light)
