Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 13 2023
 


3 Snatch Grip Push Press + 1 Overhead Squat: – Work up to a heavy complex in 10-12 minutes


GRIZZLY
– 2 sets:

6:00 AMRAP

3x50ft Shuttle Runs

10 Pull-ups

3x50ft Shuttle Runs

15 V-ups

-Rest 3:00 between sets-

* One shuttle run = 25 feet down + 25 feet back. Each full shuttle run of 50 feet counts as one rep in score


KODIAK
–
2 sets:
6:00 AMRAP
3x50ft Shuttle Runs
8 Pull-ups
3x50ft Shuttle Runs
10 V-ups
-Rest 3:00 between sets-


POLAR
– 
2 sets:
6:00 AMRAP
3x50ft Shuttle Runs
10 Ring Rows
3x50ft Shuttle Runs
15 Sit ups
-Rest 3:00 between sets-
