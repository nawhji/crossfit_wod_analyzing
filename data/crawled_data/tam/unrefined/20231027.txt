Tamalpais CrossFit WOD October 27 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 3 Rounds

10 Deadlift 155/225 M110/165

5 Bar Muscle Ups

-rest 2:00-

2 Rounds

20 Hang Power Clean 95/135 M75/105

10 Chest to Bar Pull Ups

-rest 2:00-

60 Shoulder to Overhead 65/95 M55/85

30 Pull Ups


KODIAK
– 
3 Rounds
10 Deadlift 105/155 M80/115
3 Bar Muscle Ups
-rest 2:00-
2 Rounds
20 Hang Power Clean 80/115 M65/95
8 Chest to Bar Pull Ups
-rest 2:00-
60 Shoulder to Overhead 55/75 M45/65
20 Pull Ups


POLAR
– 
3 Rounds
10 Dumbbell Deadlift (light)
5 Banded Strict Pull Ups
-rest 2:00-
2 Rounds
15 Hang Dumbbell Power Clean (light)
10 Jumping Pull Ups
-rest 2:00-
40 Dumbbell Push Press (light)
30 Ring Rows
