Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 19 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 4 sets

50 Double Unders

10 Burpee Pull Ups

50 Double Unders

3 Burpee Bar Muscle Ups

-rest 3:00 between sets-


KODIAK
-4 sets

35 Double Unders

7 Burpee Pull Ups

35 Double Unders

2 Burpee Bar Muscle Ups

-rest 3:00 between sets-


POLAR
– 4 sets

50 Single Unders

10 Sprawls + 10 Bent Over Row

50 Single Unders

10 Sprawls + 10 Jumping pull Up

-rest 3:00 between sets-


AFTER PARTY
– Leg Day 4 Rounds

10 DB Box Step-Ups

15 Seated Dumbbell Calf Raise
