Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 26 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 10 Rounds

16 Single DB Alt Step Back Lunges 35/50 M25/40

2 Wall Walks


KODIAK
– 
10 Rounds
16 Single Dumbbell Alternating Step Back Lunges 25/35 M20/30
2 Wall Walks


POLAR
– 
10 Rounds
12 Single Dumbbell Alternating Step Back Lunges (light)
2 Inch Worms


Double-Unders: Week 1 Day 1

Phase 1: Single-Unders- Practice single-unders until you can get 50 unbroken.

Goal Workout: 50 unbroken single- unders

*Once you can successfully complete this with no misses, you can move on to phase two next week.

Advanced Athlete Option: If you already have mastered double-unders, instead use this time to work on Crossovers
