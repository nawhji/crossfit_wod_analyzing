Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 9 2023
 


5 sets x 3 Bench Press @80% of 1RM


GRIZZLY
– Every 1:00 (4 rounds)

Min 1: 10/12 Calorie Bike

Min 2: 16 Kettlebell Swings 35/53

Min 3: 10/12 Calorie Row

Min 4: 50 Battle Rope

Min 5: Sled Pull to water and back 45/90

Min 6: Rest


KODIAK
– Every 1:00 (4 rounds)

Min 1: 8/10 Calorie Bike

Min 2: 16 Kettlebell Swings 26/44

Min 3: 8/10 Calorie Row

Min 4: 40 Battle Rope

Min 5: Sled Pull to water and back 45/90

Min 6: Rest


POLAR
– Every 1:00 (4 rounds)

Min 1: 6/8 Calorie Bike

Min 2: 10 Kettlebell Swings – Light

Min 3: 6/8 Calorie Row

Min 4: 30 Battle Rope

Min 5: Sled Pull 1/2 way to water and back 45/90

Min 6: Rest
