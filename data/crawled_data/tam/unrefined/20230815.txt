Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 15 2023
 


2 Snatch + 1 Overhead Squat (@70% of 1RM) x 5 sets

3 Snatch Pulls x 3 sets (@70- 80% of 1RM)

– Rest as needed between sets –


GRIZZLY
– Every 3:00 (5 sets)

60 Double Unders

10 Double Dumbbell

Snatches 35/50 M25/35


KODIAK
– Every 3:00 (5 sets)
45 Double Unders
10 Double Dumbbell Snatches (2×35/25)


POLAR
– Every 3:00 (5 sets)
50 Single Unders
8 Double Dumbbell Clean and Jerks (light)
