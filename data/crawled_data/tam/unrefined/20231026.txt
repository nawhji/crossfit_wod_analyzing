Tamalpais CrossFit WOD October 26 2023
 


Shoulder Press: – Establish a 6 RM for the day. Then, perform a drop set of 4-6 reps at 90% and 4-6 reps

85% of that 6 RM.


GRIZZLY
– Team of 3

5 Sets (each/1:2)

10/12 Calorie Bike

10 Handstand Push Ups

75ft Bodyweight Lunge


KODIAK
– 
Team of 3
5 Sets (each/1:2)
8/10 Calorie Air Bike
7 Handstand Push Ups
 75ft Bodyweight Lunge


POLAR
– 
Team of 3
5 Sets (each/1:2)
6/8 Calorie Air Bike
10 Push Ups
40ft Bodyweight Lunge
