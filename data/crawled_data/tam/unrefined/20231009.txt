Tamalpais CrossFit WOD October 9 2023
 


Hang Snatch:- Establish a 3 RM for the day

-into-

3 Snatch Deadlift x 3 Sets @90% 1RM Snatch


GRIZZLY
– 10 Rounds

30 seconds Max Rep Burpees

-Rest 30 seconds-

30 seconds Max Rep Wall

Balls 14/20

-Rest 30 seconds-

* SCORE = Total Burpees + Total Wall Balls


KODIAK
–
10 Rounds
30 seconds Max Rep Burpees
-Rest 30 seconds-
30 seconds Max Rep Wall Balls (14/10)
-Rest 30 seconds-


POLAR
– 
10 Rounds
30 seconds Max Rep Sprawls
-Rest 30 seconds-
30 seconds Max Rep Wall Ball ( light)
-Rest 30 seconds-
