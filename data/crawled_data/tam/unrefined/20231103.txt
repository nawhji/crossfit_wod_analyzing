Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 3 2023
 


Shoulder Press:- Establish a 4 RM for the day.

Then, perform a drop set of 2-4 reps at 85-90% and 2-4 reps 80-85% of that 4 RM.


GRIZZLY
– 2 Sets

In a 5:00 window

10x50ft Shuttle Run

AMRAP in the time remaining of

5 Box Jumps 24/30

5 Strict Handstand Push Ups

15 Air Squats

-rest 5 minutes between sets-


KODIAK
–
2 Sets
In a 5:00 window
10x50ft Shuttle Run
AMRAP in the time remaining of
5 Box Jumps 20/24
5 Handstand Push Ups
15 Air Squats
-rest 5 minutes between sets-


POLAR
– 
2 Sets
In a 5:00 window
6x50ft Shuttle Run
AMRAP in the time remaining of
5 Box Step Ups 
5 Push Ups
10 Air Squats
-rest 5 minutes between sets-
