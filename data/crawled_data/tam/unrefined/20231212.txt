Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 12 2023
 


1 Power Clean + 1 Below the Knee Power Clean + 1 Hang Power Clean

– Complex is completed unbroken- Work up to a heavy complex in 10-12


GRIZZLY
– 5 rounds- 35/50 M25/40

12 Dumbbell Deadlifts

9 Dumbbell Hang Cleans

6 Dumbbell Shoulder to OH

12/15 Calorie Air Bike


KODIAK
– 5 rounds- 24/35 M20/30

12 Dumbbell Deadlifts

9 Dumbbell Hang Cleans

6 Dumbbell Shoulder to Overhead

10/12 Calorie Air Bike


POLAR
– 5 rounds- Light DB’S

9 Dumbbell Deadlifts

6 Dumbbell Hang Cleans

3 Dumbbell Shoulder to Overhead

8/10 Calorie Air Bike
