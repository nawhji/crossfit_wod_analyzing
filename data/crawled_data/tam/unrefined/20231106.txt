Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 6 2023
 


GRIZZLY- 
2 Rounds

100 Double Unders

25 Overhead Squats 65/95 M55/75

100 Double Unders

25 Toes to Bar


KODIAK
– 
2 Rounds
75 Double Unders
25 Overhead Squats 55/75 M45/65
75 Double Unders
15 Toes to Bar


POLAR
– 
2 Rounds
50 Single Unders
25 Goblet Squata (light)
50 Single Unders
25 Abmat Sit Ups


CHEST AND TRICEPS-
 4 Rounds

10 Alt. DB Bench + Double DB Bench

10 Double DB Skull Crushers

-Rest 2 min between rounds-
