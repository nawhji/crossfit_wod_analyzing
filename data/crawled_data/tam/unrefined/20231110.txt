Tamalpais CrossFit WOD November 10 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 16.3- 7:00 AMRAP

10 Power Snatch 55/75 M45/65

3 Bar Muscle Ups


KODIAK
–
7:00 AMRAP
10 Power Snatch 45/65 M35/45
6 Chest to Bar


POLAR
– 
7:00 AMRAP
10 Dumbbell Snatch (light)
6 Jumping Pull Ups


FUN TIMES
–  Arms and Shoulders

4 Rounds

10 Barbell Strict Press

-Rest 2 min between rounds-
