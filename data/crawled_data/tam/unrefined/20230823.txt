Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 23 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 300ft Front Rack Carry 35/50 M25/35

-into-

5 Rounds

10 Chest To Bar Pull-Ups

10 Box Jumps Overs 20/24

-into-

300ft Front Rack Carry 35/50 M25/35


KODIA
K- 300ft Front Rack Carry 25/35 M20/30

-into-

5 Rounds

10 Pull-Ups

10 Box Jumps Overs 16/20

-into-

300ft Front Rack Carry 25/35 M20/30


POLAR
– 
300ft Farmer Carry (moderate)
-into-
5 Rounds
10 Ring Rows
10 Box Step Ups (20/16)
-into-
300ft Farmer Carry (moderate)


SKILLS AND DRILLS
 – Toes to Bar: Week 3, Day 1

– Advance: 5 sets (10:00)

8-10 Kip to Chair Position

5-8 Strict Toes to Bar

– Intermediate: 5 sets (10:00)

6-8 Kip to Chair Position

5-8 Strict Knee Raise + Extension

– Beginner: 5 sets (10:00)

5 Kip Swings + Knees up

5-8 Strict Hanging Knee Raise

-Alternative Option: 5 sets (10:00)

10 Suitcase Sit Ups

20-30-second Dead Hang (Pull Up Bar)
