Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 2 2024
 


Monday Jan 1st closed, Tuesday Jan 2nd 7am, 8am & 9:30am- Happy New Year!


GRIZZLY
– 5 rounds

9 Shoulder to OH 95/135 M75/105

12 Burpees over Bar

15 Toes to Bar


KODIAK
-5 rounds

8 Shoulder to Overhead 80/115 M65/95

10 Burpees over Bar

12 Toes to Bar


POLAR
– 5 rounds

12 Dumbbell Push Press (light)

12 Sprawls

12 Hanging Knee Raises or Sit Ups


STRENGTH
 -5 sets of 2 Turkish Get-Ups

on each side.
