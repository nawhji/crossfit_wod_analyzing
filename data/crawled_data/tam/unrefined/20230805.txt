Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 5 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 1 Mile Run

20 Muscle Ups (Bar or Ring) or 40 Burpee Pull-Ups

1 Mile Run


KODIAK- 
1200 M Run

15 Muscle Ups (Bar or Ring) or 30 Burpee Pull-Ups

1200 M Run


POLAR
– 1200 M Row

20 Sprawls + 40 Jumping Pull-Ups

1200 M Row


ACCESSORY
– 4 rounds: @ moderate weight

10 Weighted Hip Thrust

10 Alternating Double DB Step Back Lunges (Front Rack)

10 GHD Hip Ext

10 Single leg DB Romanian Deadlift (each side)

10 Single Arm KB Situps (Left)

10 Single Arm KB Situps (Right)

60 Sec Plank Hold

*Rest 3 minutes b/t rounds
