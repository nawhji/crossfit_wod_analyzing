Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 8 2023
 


Back Squat:
 Heavy 2 rep


GRIZZLY
– For time

5 Power Cleans 135/185 M95/140

15 Chest to bar Pull Ups

25 Toes to Bar

50 Wall Balls 14/20

25 Toes to Bar

15 Chest to bar Pull Ups

5 Power Cleans 135/185 M95/140


KODIAK
– For time

5 Power Cleans 115/165 M85/120

15 Pull Ups

20 Toes to Bar

50 Wall Balls 14/20

20 Toes to Bar

15 Pull Ups

5 Power Cleans 115/165 M85/120

– 
For time
5 Power Cleans (light)
10 Jumping Pull Ups
15 Hanging Knee Raises
30 Wall Ball  (light)

10 Jumping Pull Ups
5 Power Cleans (light)
