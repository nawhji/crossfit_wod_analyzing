Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 16 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 10 Sets (1 set every 2:30)

200m Run

10 Toes to Bar

3 Power Cleans 145/205 M105/155


KODIAK
–
10 Sets (1 set every 2:30)
150m Run
10 Knees to Elbows Or 7 Toes to bar
3 Power Cleans 115/165 M85/120


POLAR
– 
10 Sets (1 set every 2:30)
150m Run
10 Hanging Knee Raises
6 Sandball Slams (light)


Option 1: Gymnastics Skill- Work- Toes to Bar: Week 6 Day 2- Last day of the cycle!


Advanced & Intermediate
: 1 minute of max Kipping Toes To Bar


Beginner:
 1 minute of max Kipping Knee Raises

–rest 2 minutes-

Then complete 40% of your max number [not unbroken]

Score is reps completed in the 1 minute test


Option 2: Back and Biceps- 4 Rounds @moderate weight

10 Strict Pullups

10 Double DB Standing Bent Over Row

10 DB Lat Pull Overs

10 DB Spider Curls

-Rest 3 min b/t rounds-
