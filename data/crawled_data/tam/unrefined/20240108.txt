Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 8 2024
 


Build to a heavy Back Squat- 3 months or less, 5 reps x 3 Sets


GRIZZLY
–

30 Pull-ups

30 Back Squats 65/95 M55/75

30 Burpees

-Rest 1:00-

20 Chest to Bar

20 Back Squats 65/95 M55/75

20 Burpees with 6 inch jump

-Rest 1:00-

10 Bar Muscle Ups

10 Back Squats 65/95 M55/75

10 Burpee Over Bar


KODIAK-

30 Pull-ups

30 Back Squats 55/75 M45/65

30 Burpees

-Rest 1:00-

20 Pull-ups

20 Back Squats 55/75 M45/65

20 Burpees with 6 inch jump

-Rest 1:00-

10 Pull-ups

10 Back Squats 55/75 M45/65

10 Burpee Over Bar


POLAR-

20 Bent Over Rows

20 Air or Goblet Squats

20 Sprawls

-Rest 1:00-

15 Bent Over Flies

15 Air or Goblet Squats

15 Sprawls

-Rest 1:00-

10 Plank Rows

10 Air or Goblet Squat

10 Sprawls
