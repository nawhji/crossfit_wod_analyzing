Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 28 2023
 


GRIZZLY
– 2 sets (1 set every 10:00)

16/20 Calorie Air Bike

20 V-Ups

25 Alt DB Snatches 35/50 M25/40

16/20 Calorie Air Bike

20 V-Ups


KODIAK
– 
2 sets (1 set every 10:00)
13/16 Calorie Air Bike
15 V-Ups
25 Alternating Dumbbell Snatches 25/35 M20/30
13/16 Calorie Air Bike
15 V-Ups


POLAR
– 
2 sets (1 set every 10:00)
10/12 Calorie Air Bike
16 Sit Ups
20 Alternating Dumbbell Snatches (light)
10/12 Calorie Air Bike
16 Sit Ups


Fun Times
– 4 Rounds

12 Plate Front Raises

10 Standing Tricep DB French press
