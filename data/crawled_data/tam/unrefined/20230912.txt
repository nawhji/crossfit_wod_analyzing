Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 12 2023
 


10 sets: 1 Squat Snatch (start at 65% and build, no higher than 90%)

* Complete 1 set every 1:15 *


GRIZZLY
– 2 sets:

21 Box Jump Overs 20/24

12/15 Calorie Row

9 Burpee Box Get Overs 24/30

21 Box Jump Overs 20/24

12/15 Calorie Row

9 Burpee Box Get Overs 24/30

-rest until 10:00, perform second set reversed-


KODIAK
– 
2 sets:
21 Box Jump Overs 16/20
10/12
 Calorie Row
9 Burpee Box Get Overs 20/24
21 Box Jump Overs 16/20
10/12 Calorie Row
9 Burpee Box Get Overs 20/24
-rest until 10:00, perform second set reversed-


POLAR
– 
2 sets:
21 Box Step Ups 
8/12 Calorie Row
9 Sprawl Box Step ups 
21 Box Step Ups 
8/10 alorie Row
9 Sprawl Box Step ups
-rest until 10:00, perform second set reversed-
