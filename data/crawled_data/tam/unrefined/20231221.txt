Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 21 2023
 


GRIZZLY & KODIAK
– Part A:

For Time:

Max Effort, 500m Row

-Rest till 10:00, then Part B-

Part B:

Death by Shuttle Runs

* Each shuttle run = 25 feet down + 25 feet back (50 feet total)


POLAR
– Part A:

For Time:

Max Effort, 300m Row

-Rest till 10:00-

Part B:

Death by Shuttle Runs (25ft)


AFTER PARTY
– 3 Rounds

12 Double DB Prone Row

12 Inverted Skull Crusher
