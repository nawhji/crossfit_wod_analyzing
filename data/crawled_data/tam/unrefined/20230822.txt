Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 22 2023
 


5 sets: 1 High Hang Clean + 1 Hang Clean + 1 Clean (@65% of 1RM Clean)

* Complete a set every 1:30 *

-Into-

3 sets: 3 Push Jerks (@75% of 1RM Clean and Jerk)


GRIZZLY
– “The Chief” 3:00 AMRAP (5 sets)

3 Power Cleans 95/135 M75/105

6 Push Ups

9 Air Squats

-Rest 1:00 between sets-


KODIAK
–
3:00 AMRAP (5 sets)
3 Power Cleans 80/115 M65/95
6 Push Ups
9 Air Squats
-Rest 1:00 between sets-


POLAR
– 
3:00 AMRAP (5 sets)
3 Dumbbell Power Cleans (light)
6 Bar Push Ups
9 Air Squats
-Rest 1:00 between sets-
