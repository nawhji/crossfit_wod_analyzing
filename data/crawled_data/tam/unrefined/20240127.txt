Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 27 2024
 


GRIZZLY-
 Teams of 2- 15 Rounds (each)

Partner 1:

3 Strict pull Ups or 4 Kipping

2 Wall Walks

Partner 2:

8/10 Calorie Air Bike

-Both athletes work at the same time. Switch when both are completed-


KODIAK
– Teams of 2- 15 Rounds (each)

Partner 1:

2 Strict pull Ups or 3 Kipping

1 Wall Walk

Partner 2:

8/10 Calorie Air Bike


POLAR
– Teams of 2- 15 Rounds (each)

Partner 1:

6 Bent Over Rows

3 Inch Worms

Partner 2:

6/8 Calorie Air Bike


ARMS AND SHOULDERS
– 4 rounds:

10 Standing Alternating Double DB Shoulder Press (each side)

10 Bent Over Lateral Raises

10 Standing KB Crush Grip Bicep Curl

10 Tricep Dips
