Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 30 2024
 


5 sets of 5 Back Squats @75% of Heavy Single (Week 1)

* Complete on the 2 min x 10 minutes.


GRIZZLY
– 5 Rounds

10 Lateral Burpee Over Dumbbell

50ft Single Dumbbell Walking Lunge 35/50 M25/40

16/20 Calorie Air Bike


KODIAK
– 5 Rounds

8 Lateral Burpee Over Dumbbell

50ft Single Dumbbell Walking Lunge 25/35 M20/30

13/16 Calorie Air Bike

– 5 Rounds

8 Sprawls

50ft Walking Lunge
