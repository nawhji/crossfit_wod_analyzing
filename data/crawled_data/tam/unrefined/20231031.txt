Tamalpais CrossFit WOD October 31 2023
 


SKILLS AND DRILLS Double-Unders: Week 6 Day 1 Final test day!!!

5 min to find one set of max unbroken double-unders (advanced: unbroken cross-overs)


GRIZZLY
– 6 Rounds

10 Toes to Bar

5/5 Hang Kettlebell Snatch 35/53

40 Double Unders


KODIAK
–
6 Rounds
8 Toes to Bar
5/5 Hang Kettlebell Snatch 26/35
30 Double Unders


POLAR
– 
6 Rounds
10 Hanging Knee Raises
5/5 Hang Dumbbell Snatch (light)
40 Single Unders
