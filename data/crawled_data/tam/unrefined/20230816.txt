Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 16 2023
 


2 Power Cleans + 1 Push Jerk x 5 sets (@65% of 1RM)

3-2-1-1-1 Front Squat (build up in weight)

– Rest as needed between sets –


GRIZZLY
– Every 5:00 (3 sets)

24/30 Calorie Assault Bike or 400 M Run

Max Unbroken Handstand Push Ups


KODIAK
– 
Every 5:00 (3 sets)
20/24 Calorie Assault Bike
Max Unbroken Handstand Push Ups


POLAR
– 
Every 5:00 (3 sets)
16/20 Calorie Assault Bike
Max Unbroken Push Ups
