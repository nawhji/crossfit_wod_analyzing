Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 22 2024
 


Every Minute (10:00) 5 Power Clean and Push Jerks (+5lbs total from Wk1)

– Use lightweight that can be cycled smoothly for 5 T&G


GRIZZLY
– 15:00 AMRAP

50ft Overhead Dumbbell Walking Lunge (R)

15 Toes to Bar

50ft Overhead Dumbbell Walking Lunge (L)

15 Burpee over Dumbbell

15 Dumbbell Snatch 35/50 M25/40


KODIAK
-15:00 AMRAP

50ft Overhead Dumbbell Walking Lunge (R)

12 Toes to bar

50ft Overhead Dumbbell Walking Lunge (L)

12 Burpee over Dumbbell

12 Dumbbell Snatch 25/35 M20/30


POLAR
– 15:00 AMRAP

50ft Dumbbell Walking Lunge (light)

10 Hanging Knee Raises

50ft Dumbbell Walking Lunge (light)

10 Sprawls

10 Dumbbell Snatch (light)
