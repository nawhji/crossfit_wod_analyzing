Tamalpais CrossFit WOD September 5 2023
 


5 sets: 1 High Hang Power Snatch + 1 Hang Power Snatch + 1 Power Snatch (@70% of 1RM Snatch)

* Complete a set every 1:30 *

-into-

3 sets: 2 Overhead Squats (@80% of 1RM Snatch)

* Rest as needed between sets *


GRIZZLY
– 24/30 Calorie Row

30 Back Squats 105/155 M80/115

16/20 Calorie Row

20 Back Squats 105/155 M80/115


KODIAK
– 20/24 Calorie Row

30 Back Squats 75/105 M65/95

12/16 Calorie Row

20 Back Squats 75/105 M65/95


POLAR
-16
/20 Calorie Row
30 Dumbbell Front Squats (light)
8/10 Calorie Row
20 Dumbbell Front Squats (light)
