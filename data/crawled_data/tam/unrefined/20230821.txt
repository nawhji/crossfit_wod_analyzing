Tamalpais CrossFit WOD August 21 2023
 


5 sets: 1 High Hang Power

Snatch + 1 Hang Power

Snatch + 1 Power Snatch

(@65% of 1RM Snatch)

* Complete a set every 1:30 *


3 sets: 3 OHS

(@75% of 1RM Snatch)

* Rest as needed between sets *


GRIZZLY
– 5:00 AMRAP

200 Double Unders

In the remaining time, complete max Distance SAOH Walking Lunge 25/35 M20/30 (score is total distance lunged)

-rest 2 minutes –

5:00 AMRAP

Complete 200ft SAOH Walking Lunge

In the remaining time, complete max Double Unders


KODIAK
–
5:00 AMRAP
150 Double Unders
In the remaining time, complete max Distance Single Arm Overhead
Walking Lunge (25/15).
-rest 2 minutes –
5:00 AMRAP
Complete 200ft Single Arm Overhead Walking Lunge (25/15)
In the remaining time, complete max Double Unders


POLAR
– 
5:00 AMRAP
200 Single Unders
In the remaining time, complete max Dumbbell Step ups (light).
-rest 2 minutes –
5:00 AMRAP
Complete 30 Dumbbell Step Ups (light)
In the remaining time, complete max Single Unders
