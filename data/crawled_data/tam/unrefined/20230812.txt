Option 1: Gymnastics Skill
 Work

Toes to Bar: Week 1 Day 2

10-20-30-20-10

Advanced/Intermediate: Kip Swings

Beginner: Kip Swings on Rings

Advanced/Intermediate: 10 V- Ups on Floor
