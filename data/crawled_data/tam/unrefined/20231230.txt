Tamalpais CrossFit WOD December 30 2023
 


Holiday Class Schedule- Monday Jan 1st closed, Tuesday Jan 2nd 7am, 8am & 9:30am- Happy New Year!


All Bears
– Teams of 3- 7 Rnds- 3:00 on 1 min off-

Athlete 1: Max Reps of Medball Clean to Wall Ball

Athlete 2: Plank Hold

Athlete 3: Row

– Both Athlete 1 & 2 Must be working for athlete #3 to Row

– Rotate and Rest as desired.


AFTER PARTY
– 3 Rounds

10 Banded Pushups

10 Single Arm KB Situps (Left)

10 Single Arm KB Situps (Right)

100ft KB Overhead Walk (50L/50R)
