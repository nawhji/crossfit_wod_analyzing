Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 26 2024
 


GRIZZLY
– 25 Shuttle Runs (50ft)

50 Wall Balls 14/20

50 V-Ups

50 Wall Balls

25 Shuttle Runs (50ft)


KODIAK
– 25 Shuttle Runs (50ft)

35 Wall Balls 14/20

35 V-Ups

35 Wall Balls

25 Shuttle Runs


POLAR
– 15 Shuttle Runs (50ft)

30 Wall Balls (light)

30 Sit Ups

30 Wall Balls (light)

15 Shuttle Runs


Core Work: 4 sets:

7 Alligator Rolls (each way)

20 Plank Kettlebell Pull Unders

30 yd Iso DB Farmers Carry (left)

30 yd Iso DB Farmers Carry (right)

*Rest 2:00 between sets
