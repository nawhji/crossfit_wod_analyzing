Tamalpais CrossFit WOD January 16 2024
 


5 Back Squat x 5 sets @65% of Heavy Single (Week 1)

* Complete a set on the 2 minutes *


GRIZZLY
– 15:00 AMRAP

10 Toes to Bar

25ft Handstand Walk Or 3 Wall Walks

10 Hang Power Cleans 65/95 M55/75


KODIAK
– 15:00 AMRAP

8 Toes to Bar

15ft Handstand Walk (Or 2 Wall Walks)

8 Hang Power Cleans 65/95 M55/75


POLAR
– 15:00 AMRAP

10 Hang Knee Raises

25ft Bear Crawl

10 Hang Dumbbell Cleans (light)
