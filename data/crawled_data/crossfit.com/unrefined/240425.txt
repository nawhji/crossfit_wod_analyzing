“Watch Nicole Christensen Coach a CrossFit Class”


One of the best ways to grow as a coach is to watch other coaches and analyze what they do throughout each section of a class. Follow along in this video as Nicole Christensen (CF-L4) coaches a class from start to finish.


Interested in pursuing a career as a CrossFit coach?


The CrossFit Level 1 Certificate Course is the first step in becoming a coach. This two-day, in person course offers expert instruction on the CrossFit methodology through classroom instruction, small-group training sessions, and coach-led workouts. The knowledge you'll gain from the Level 1 Course will set you up for a path of continued education through the Level 2, 3, and 4 credentials.
